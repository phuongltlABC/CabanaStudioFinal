Policies

1. Orders & Payments

We offer multiple payment options to make your shopping experience convenient and secure.

Accepted Payment Methods:
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): Available for orders ₫500,000+.
  • Internet Banking & Credit/Debit Cards: Secure and fast transactions.
  • E-wallets: Supports MoMo, ZaloPay, and more.
  • Postpaid Payment: Orders will be reserved for 24 hours pending payment confirmation.
Important Terms:
  • Orders must be paid within 24 hours if using postpaid payment; otherwise, they will be automatically canceled.
  • Orders can be canceled before packaging. Once packaged, they can only be exchanged or returned.
  • For COD orders, customers are required to be available to receive and pay upon delivery.
2. Shipping & Delivery

We ensure fast and reliable delivery through our trusted partners: Viettel Post, GHN, GHTK, DHL Express, and AhaMove.

Delivery Time Estimates:
  • Same-city deliveries: Same-day or within 24 hours (AhaMove, GHN, GHTK).
  • Domestic shipping: 2-5 days depending on location (Viettel Post, GHN, GHTK).
  • International shipping: 3-10 days depending on the region (DHL Express).
Shipping Fees:
  • Free delivery within Ho Chi Minh City for orders ₫2,000,000+.
  • Shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on the carrier’s pricing.
  • Additional fees may apply for bulky items or express delivery.
 3. Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you need to return or exchange an item, please review our policy below.

Eligibility for Returns & Exchanges:
  • Items can be returned or exchanged within 7 days from the delivery date.
  • The item must be unused, undamaged, and in its original packaging.
  • Eligible reasons for return/exchange:
    • Manufacturer defects or quality issues.
    • Incorrect item received.
 4. Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. We ensure secure transactions and protect your personal information.

Data Protection:
  • Personal details are encrypted and never shared with third parties.
  • Payment information is secured with industry-standard encryption.
  • We do not store credit card details after transactions.
Your Rights:
  • You may request data deletion at any time.
  • We comply with all applicable data protection laws to safeguard your personal information.
